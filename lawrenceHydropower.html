Lawrence Hydropower

History of Lawrence Hydropower

In 1874 Lawrence Kansas residents had burned all the region's wood, and fossil fuels were not available. Orlando Darling built a dam across the Kansas River north of town and strung mechanical cables from a water wheel to power local industries. Justin Bowersock bought the operation, naming it "The Bowersock Mills & Power Company" (Bowersock). Bowersock's slogan is: "http://www.bowersockpower.com" Producing clean, renewable hydropower since 1874
